Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24 September 2025

ExpensePilot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. We understand that your financial information is sensitive, and we treat it with the utmost care and respect.

How We Handle Your Data

Offline-First Architecture: ExpensePilot is designed with privacy at its core. Your expense data, budgets, and transactions are stored locally on your device by default. We don't access, collect, or process your financial data on our servers unless you explicitly choose to enable cloud sync.

Cloud Sync (Optional): If you choose to enable cloud backup, your data is securely encrypted and stored using Google Firebase. This is entirely optional and at your discretion.

What Data We Collect

  • Locally Stored Data: Expenses, budgets, categories, and preferences remain on your device
  • Cloud Data (if enabled): Encrypted backups of your financial data
  • Play Store Data: Basic device identifiers for app licensing

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal or financial data with third parties. Information may only be shared with trusted service providers (Google Firebase, Google Play) or when legally required.

Data Security

Your data security is our priority. We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures. Local data is encrypted using your device's built-in security. Cloud data uses end-to-end encryption.

Data Deletion & Account Management

Full Control: You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time directly through the in-app Delete Account option in Settings. Once deleted, all synced data is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.

Local Data: Data stored on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app or through your device's settings.

Third-Party Services

Google Firebase: Securely stores encrypted backups if you enable cloud sync.
Google Play Billing: Handles subscription payments securely.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy here and updating the effective date. Your continued use of the app indicates acceptance.

Contact Us

If you have privacy concerns or questions, please contact us at:
Email: support@expensepilot.in
Response Time: We typically respond within 7 business days.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access your data, request deletion, opt-out of data processing, and data portability.

ExpensePilot respects your privacy. Your trust is our most valuable asset.
