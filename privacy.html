Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 24 September 2025

ExpensePilot ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This App primarily stores data locally on your device. If you choose to enable cloud sync, data is securely stored using Firebase services.

We do not sell your data. Information may only be shared with trusted service providers (e.g., Google Firebase, Play Billing) or when required by law.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated cloud data at any time directly through the in-app Delete Account option is Settings. Once deleted, all synced data—such as backups, expenses, and profile details—is permanently removed from our servers.

Local data stored on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app.

If you need assistance with deletion or have privacy-related questions, you may contact us at support@expensepilot.in.
